our mission is simple: plant more trees.

deforestation significantly disrupts ecosystems and contributes towards the issues of climate change.

it destroys habitats, causes landslides, and increases flooding risks.

at more trees, we take all donations with the goal of revitalizing habitats and allowing forests to flourish.
