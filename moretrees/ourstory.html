our story

at more trees, we believe in the power of nature to heal itself--if only we give it a helping hand. our journey began with simple, urgent mission: restore forests and give life back to habitats and landscapes devastated by the effects of deforestation and climate change.

how it started

our founds witnessed the impact of deforestation first hand: vanishing forests, displaced wildlife, and communities struggling to find a way to cope with the effects of environmental degradation. instead of standing by, we decided to take action. we started small, gathering a few volunteers who were ready to travel the world, raise funds, and plant trees in affected areas. what began as a small, local effort quickly grew into a movement.

what we do

today, more trees works with passionate volunteers, generous donors, and local communities to keep doing what we love and restore forests where they're needed most. currently, our volunteers are located in belize, brazil, indonesia, and congo. through hands-on reforestation projects, we plant trees that will grow into thriving ecosystems, providing new homes for wildlife, purifying the air, and stabilizing our planet's climate.

the power of people

our success isn't just measured in the number of trees we plant--it's about the people who make it possible. volunteers dig deep, both in the soil and in their hearts, to be part of something bigger. donors fuel our mission, ensuring we have the tools and resources needed to keep planting.

join the movement

we believe that every tree planted is a step toward a healthier planet. whether you give time, resources, or simply help spread the word, our team at more trees would like to thank you for making a difference. together, we can regrow what was lost, restore balance to nature, and ensure that future generations inherit a world full of more trees.

let's plant hope. let's plant change. let's plant more trees.
